Welcome to Quest For Health

Quest For Health is an online browser role play game to help people lead a healthy life.

This is achieved through quests that are to be performed. The quests are, for example, general exercise quests, such as walking or running. If a treating physician uses this application, the physician can also assign quests. For example, in the case of physiotherapy, treatment is improved when exercises are completed.

For completed quests, one receives experience points and gold. The gold received can be used to buy and equip items that give strength to one's character. With the strength, a coherent story is told.
